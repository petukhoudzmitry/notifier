REGISTRATION IN THE LIVE QUEUE

11/11/2025

Starting14 November 2025, registration for new participants in theLive Queuewill reopen. Inquiry submission period: from 14 November 2025. To obtain a number on theLive Queuewaiting list, please send an email toLQ2it@tlscontact.comwith the following details for each customer: 

  • Surname and first name (in Latin letters, as shown in your passport) 

  • Date of birth 

  • Phone number (format: 375 XX XXXXXXX) 

  • Email address 

  • Group number / M-number (your online account must be completed and confirmed) 

  • An account can be created for any region (Minsk, Brest). 

After submission of correct inquiry, you will receive a reply with aninvitation for in-person verificationat the Visa Application Centre in Minsk with the scheduled date and time. Upon successful verification, you will be assigned aLive Queue number. 

Important:Incomplete or incorrect inquiries will be rejected. 

You are eligible for a live queue number only if you have no valid Schengen visa or if your visa expires in 3 months or less at the time of on-site verification.  

For full details, please visit theFAQsection Live Queue general information.
