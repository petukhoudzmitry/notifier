Booking session types in 2026

12/16/2025

In response to the numerous wishes of the "Live Queue" participants for an accelerated and comfortable application through the Premium Service, some of the slots in the VIP lounge will be given for this purpose. Starting in January 2026, there will be three (3) types of manual booking sessions for “Live Queue” Participants:

 1. Standard slots

 2. Prime Time Slots

 3. Premium Slots (VIP lounge)

Session announcements will be posted in the News section with specific ranges of “Live Queue” numbers and application dates. The range of numbers is defined by TLScontact. Without a number in the waiting list "Premium Service" or "Live Queue", service in the VIP lounge is not provided. Acceptance of new applications for the "Premium Service" waiting list is not yet planned.
